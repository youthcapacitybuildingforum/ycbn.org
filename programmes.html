Our Programmes

What We Do

We deliver sustainable WASH infrastructure and empower youth through technology and innovation, creating resilient communities across Uganda.

Our Thematic Areas

We focus on two core pillars that drive sustainable community transformation.

Water, Sanitation & Hygiene (WASH)

Sustainable WASH interventions designed to improve public health outcomes, school attendance, and community resilience.

Key Interventions:

  • Drilling and rehabilitation of boreholes and community wells
  • Construction of gender-responsive and disability-inclusive sanitation facilities
  • School-based hygiene education programs
  • Menstrual Hygiene Management (MHM) support for vulnerable girls
  • Formation and training of community water management committees
Strategic Outcome:

Improved access to safe water and sanitation services for underserved communities and schools, contributing to enhanced health, dignity, and educational retention.

Innovation, Technology & Youth Empowerment

Digital transformation and skills training to equip young people with practical, market-relevant competencies.

Key Interventions:

  • Training youth in digital skills (AI fundamentals, coding, agritech, fintech)
  • Establishment of community innovation hubs
  • Organization of hackathons to address WASH and localized development challenges
  • Promotion of low-cost innovations such as solar-powered water systems
  • Incubation and mentorship support for youth-led start-ups
Strategic Outcome:

Youth equipped with employable digital competencies and empowered to design technology-driven solutions that respond to community needs.

Solid Waste Management

Empowering communities to manage waste sustainably, promoting a cleaner environment, healthier living, and economic opportunities through responsible waste practices.

Waste Segregation

Training communities on sorting organic, recyclable, and hazardous waste at source.

Recycling & Upcycling

Creating income-generating activities through recycling and upcycling initiatives.

Community Awareness

Campaigns and workshops to promote proper waste disposal and environmental stewardship.
