Who We Are

Youth Capacity Building Network (YCBN) is a youth-led development organization established to address critical gaps in Water, Sanitation and Hygiene (WASH) and youth-driven innovation in Uganda.

We integrate sustainable infrastructure development with digital skills training and entrepreneurship support to create resilient communities and economically empowered youth. Our focus is on underserved rural and peri-urban communities, with a strategic emphasis on schools and vulnerable populations.

Safe Water Access

Drilling and rehabilitating boreholes and community wells for clean water.

Solid Waste Management

We empower communities to manage waste sustainably, promoting a cleaner environment, healthier living, and economic opportunities through responsible waste practices.

Digital Skills

AI fundamentals, coding, agritech, and fintech training for youth.

Innovation Hubs

Community innovation spaces and startup incubation support.

Our Vision

"A nation where every community has access to safe water, dignified sanitation, resilient infrastructure, and youth-driven innovations that accelerate sustainable development."

Our Mission

To improve quality of life by delivering sustainable WASH infrastructure, strengthening educational environments, advancing hygiene and menstrual health systems, and equipping youth with emerging technologies to solve local challenges.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every action we take and every community we serve.

Integrity & Transparency

Operating with honesty and openness in all our dealings and financial management.

Community Ownership

Ensuring communities lead and sustain the solutions we implement together.

Sustainability

Building long-lasting solutions that continue to benefit communities for generations.

Innovation

Embracing creative approaches and emerging technologies to solve local challenges.

Inclusion & Equity

Ensuring no one is left behind, with special focus on vulnerable populations.

Accountability

Taking responsibility for our actions and delivering on our commitments.

Our Strategic Objectives

1

Sustainable WASH Infrastructure

To deliver sustainable, inclusive WASH infrastructure that guarantees safe water and dignified sanitation for underserved communities.

2

Community Hygiene Systems

Institutionalize comprehensive community hygiene and menstrual health systems that improve health, dignity, and educational outcomes.

3

Youth Workforce Development

To build a highly skilled, innovation-driven youth workforce equipped for employment and entrepreneurship.

4

Technology Solutions

Catalyse scalable, youth-led technological solutions that transform local social and environmental challenges into sustainable enterprises.
